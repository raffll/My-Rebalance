# Remastered Rebalance Redux

------------------------------------------------------------

## Goals

- Appreciate Morrowind randomness and not disabling anything; if you want to cheese, you still can!
- Everything here is modified, having Tamriel Rebuilt in mind, and Tamriel Data is a requirement.
- Make progression slower to become op much later.
- Move fight balance from health + offensive spells to using defensive spells.
- Make enchanting a useful skill
- Make security a useful skill
- Make user-made potions less powerful and modify store potions to be on par.
- Fix economy
- Make pickpocketing possible
-

------------------------------------------------------------

## Core

- Halves the health gain per level-up. Now at level 70 you will have around 300-400 HP.
- Halves skill progression rate for all skills.
- Moves Armorer to Endurance. Strength now has 4 attributes from 5, and Endurance has 4 from 3.
- Increases walk speeds by 25% for all characters and creatures.
- Extends merchant gold reset delay to one month. I believe this will fix the economy by itself by reducing the amount of gold in the market.
- Raises Mages Guild travel cost 5 times.
- Reduces Creeper's and Mudcrab's gold.
- Raises bounties for crimes. This was 
- Raises max pickpocket chance to 100% and allows more expensive items to be stolen.
- Increases lockpick difficulty.
- Doubles the cost of Open spells.
- Rebalances Open spell magnitudes across tiers.
- Introduces trap cost multiplier
- increases the gold cost of all common and master trap types.
- Halves potion strength and raises Secret Master apparatus prices 5× to reflect their power.
- Disables automatic enchantment recharging and reduces enchantment success chance by 80%.

------------------------------------------------------------

## Magic

- Magic effects rebalance.
- Make pre-made potions to follow the 1-to-3 ratio.
    - Bargain and Cheap potions improved
    - Introduces new tiers for pre-made potions to better match with custom-made equivalents.
- Spells adjusted to new magic effects
    - If the effect was reduced 3x, then spells will be 3x stronger, keeping same amount of mana to cast
    - An exception to this rule is when the spell was too weak (1 mana cost or 5s duration) or strong initially.
    - Another exception is when there is a very similar spell already.
    - Or sometimes to improve spell progression e.g if there is a gap in spell strength
- Increases the Elemental Shield damage reflection multiplier.
