## Notes

- "Rising Force" potions use the **Levitate** effect, not Swift Swim.
- TD potions with `T_Nor_` prefix come in Cheap/Quality tiers only.
- TD potions with `T_Com_` prefix come in full Bargain–Exclusive tiers.
- Paralysis Resistance potions have non-standard tier values (7/10, 10/20, 15/45, 20/60, 30/90).
- Some attribute names are shortened in IDs: Intell, Personal, Willpow (Fortify), Enduranc, Intelli, Personal, Willpow (Restore).
