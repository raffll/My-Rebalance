## Notes

- **Cost** = R3 spell cost formula using R3 base costs and R3-modified magnitudes/durations where applicable.
- **Src**: V = vanilla Morrowind, TD = Tamriel Data.
- Identical duplicates (vanilla + TD version) shown as (×2).
- Utility effects (Mark, Recall, Cure, Intervention) have near-zero formula cost but high gameplay value — extreme ratios are expected.
- Summon/Bound effects use duration-based cost: (1+1) × dur × (base/40).
- Scrolls in the "Underpriced" band have enchantments worth far more than their gold price (good player value).
- Scrolls in "Very Overpriced" and "Extreme" bands cost much more gold than their enchantment power suggests.
