## Notes

- "Rising Force" potions use the **Levitate** effect.
- Bargain Potion of Swift Swim has 1pt magnitude (not 5pt like other Bargains) — vanilla inconsistency.
- Exclusive Frost Shield has 30s duration (not 60s like other Exclusives) — vanilla inconsistency.
- Vanilla Feather potions skip Standard tier (no p_feather_s exists).
- Vanilla Swift Swim potions skip Standard tier (no p_swift_swim_s exists).
- Restore Attribute potions have duration 0 (instant restore).
- Standard Fire Resistance has a space in the ID: `p_fire resistance_s` (vanilla typo).
- Lightning Shield IDs have a space: `p_lightning shield_b` etc.
