## Loot

**Dremora**: Daedra Heart + random weapon (Dwarven at low levels, Ebony at 14+, Daedric at 16+)

**Golden Saint**: Random weapon (Glass/Ebony/Daedric) + random shield (includes Daedric Tower Shield)

**Greater Bonewalker**: 0-5 Bonemeal + random loot

**Skeletal Minion**: Iron Shield + Bonemeal + random Iron weapon
